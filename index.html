How To Play Aim Trainer?

Press Space or shoot a target to start the timer,
The timer stops as soon as all the targets are hit.

Hiting the target in the center deals more damage :)

How to reload?

Press R to reload weapon.
Note: Weapons can't be fired while reloading

Settings

Number of targets to hit
Number of targets: Min limit is 1 and Max is 100.
Mag bullet count
Number of bullets stored in a Mag: Min limit is 6 and Max is 999.
